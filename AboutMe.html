Welcome to Shrirang Cooks!

Hi! Welcome to Shrirang Cooks where I show you some of my favorite recipes, each one unique and absolutely delectable. I’m Shrirang, the guy who cooks, and I’m here to share with you guys some of the amazing recipes I have made throughout my life.

My journey with food started over quarantine where I had far too much free time on my hands. Over the long quarantine I tried exploring many creative things to pass my time such as drawing, painting, etc., but none of them clicked with me the way making food did. It started with my dad’s birthday where we couldn’t get a cake for him due to social distancing restrictions. I felt really bad about this since what’s a birthday without a cake, so I decided to make him one.

Since that one birthday I have made many more foods and not just cakes, but so many other delicious recipes. All of my recipes are vegetarian and eggless and are simple enough for any level of cook to enjoy making and eating. I hope that through this website I can inspire everyone to cherish food as much as I do and I hope you guys are ready to do some cooking!
