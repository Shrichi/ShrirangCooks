Pesto Gnocchi

What is it?

This pesto gnocchi makes a perfect meal for any occasion, the rich, creamy pesto combined with the tender and fluffy gnocchi gives a 5 start expiriene with just a few simple ingredients.

Ingredients:

Pesto

  • 2 cups basil
  • 1/2 cup parmesean cheese*
  • 2-3 cloves of garlic, or to taste
  • 1/2 cup toasted pine nuts or cashews
  • 1/4 cup olive oil, adjust for consistency
Other cheeses work but parmesean is best

Gnocchi

  • 3-4 medium potatoes
  • 2-3 cups flour, adjust until desired consistency
  • 1 tsp olive oil for boiling
  • Pinch of salt for boiling
  • Optional tomatoes for garnish

Recipe

Pesto

  1. Add all the ingredients to a blender but leave half the oil
  2. Blend fully and keep adding oil until desired consistency is reached

Gnocchi

  1. Boil and peel all of your potatoes
  2. Rice all of your potatoes into a large mixing bowl
  3. Add flour 1/4 cup at a time until a dough forms
  4. Shape your dough into small pieces of gnocchi of any desired shape, make sure the sizes are equal for easy boiling
  5. Bring water to a boild with a tsp of olive oil and a pinch of salt
  6. Add the gnocchi and gently stir for about 2-3 minutes and take out
  7. Add pesto to a pan with a splash of the pasta water if needed to thin
  8. Add gnocchi and mix for just a minute until well incorporated
  9. Add desried garnish such as cheese or tomatoes and enjoy!
